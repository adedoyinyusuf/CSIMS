### **UX Design Hierarchy Strategy**

#### **1. Administrative Authority**
- **Primary**: Lapis Lazuli + Paynes Gray gradients
- **Psychology**: Deep, professional colors establish administrative authority
- **Usage**: Admin navigation, headers, system controls

#### **2. Member Friendliness** 
- **Primary**: Columbia Blue + Alice Blue combinations
- **Psychology**: Light, approachable colors create welcoming member experience
- **Usage**: Member dashboards, forms, content areas

#### **3. Action Hierarchy**
- **Primary Actions**: Persian Orange (warm, inviting)

#### **4. Status Communication**

### **Accessibility Considerations**
- All combinations tested for WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- High contrast maintained between blues and oranges/reds
- Color-blind friendly palette with sufficient luminance differences
- Clear semantic meaning through color psychology
