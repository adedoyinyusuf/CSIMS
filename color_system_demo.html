Color Psychology & Professional Impact

Blue-Gray Foundation: Establishes trust, reliability, and professional credibility. These colors communicate stability and competence, essential for financial and membership management systems.

Orange Accents: Strategic use of warm orange tones for calls-to-action and status communication. Orange conveys energy, enthusiasm, and draws attention to important interactive elements.

Sophisticated Neutrals: Charcoal and Paynes Gray provide professional depth and ensure excellent readability while maintaining a modern, sophisticated appearance.

Status Colors: Carefully selected status colors (success green, warning amber, error red) provide clear visual feedback while maintaining accessibility standards.
